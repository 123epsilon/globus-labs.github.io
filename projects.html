Projects

RAMSES
The RAMSES project aims to develop a new science of end-to-end analytical performance modeling that will transform understanding of the behavior of science workflows in extreme-scale science environments.

Data Search
There is a vast amount of scientific data distributed across storage systems and data repositories. We are developing methods to crawl, extract metadata, and index huge amounts of scientific data.

Information Extraction
A wealth of valuable data is locked within the millions of research articles published each year. We are researching methods to liberate this data via hybrid human-machine models.
